About ResQPaws

Learn more about our mission to help injured animals get the care they need faster.

What is ResQPaws?

ResQPaws is a platform that helps people report injured animals and connect them with nearby help to enable faster rescue and care.

How ResQPaws Works

ResQPaws follows a simple flow:

  1. A user joins the platform and becomes a “hero”.
  2. The user reports an injured or abandoned animal by sharing basic details like location and condition.
  3. The information helps nearby volunteers, rescuers, or animal welfare organizations respond more quickly.
  4. Faster information leads to faster care and better chances of rescue.

Why ResQPaws Is Needed

Many animals suffer not because people don’t care, but because:

  • There is no clear reporting system
  • Help does not reach the right people on time
  • Information is scattered or delayed

ResQPaws exists to reduce this delay by creating a simple, centralized reporting and connection system.

Who ResQPaws Is For

ResQPaws can be used by:

  • Anyone who sees an animal in need
  • Animal lovers who want to help
  • Volunteers and rescuers
  • Animal welfare organizations
