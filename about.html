Our Mission

Build a simple, reliable rescue reporting bridge so no injured animal is ignored due to delay or missing information.

Our Vision

A city-wide safety net where anyone can trigger rapid response with just a photo, location, and urgency level.

Who Can Use ResQPaws

  • Citizens who spot animals in distress
  • Rescue volunteers and transport teams
  • Animal welfare organizations and shelters
  • Students and communities who want to help

How It Works

  1. Upload photo + add location
  2. Select urgency (regular, urgent, critical)
  3. Nearby NGOs receive actionable report details
  4. Case status updates stay visible to the reporter

Rescue Flow

1 Report Submitted

2 NGO Assigned

3 On-Ground Response

4 Animal Stabilized

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an account to report an emergency?

No. Emergency reporting works without login so help can be requested immediately.

What details should I include?

Photo, exact location, and short condition notes. These three details improve response speed most.

Can I track what happens after submitting?

Yes. Logged-in users can view updates like accepted, in-progress, and rescued status.
